conditions and requirements

CONDITIONS: with your own car:
  • Connect to the UBER system for 10%
  • No commissions 0%!
  • We can connect remotely without coming to the office!
  • IP is not required
  • Orders are distributed without taking into account the rating to the nearest drivers
  • Work only by non-cash settlement.
  • We transfer the money to the driver's card or in cash at the office
Earnings per day are 3,000-6,000 UAH + a bonus of 1,000 UAH
CONDITIONS: on company cars:
  • We provide a car without a deposit
  • No daily rent payments
  • Wednesday, Friday and Saturday you work for the company, the rest of the days you work for yourself
REQUIREMENT:
  • Availability of a car without advertising
  • The car must not be yellow and not have yellow license plates
  • The car is not older than 3 years
  • Driving experience of at least 3 years
  • The device is based on Android 4.0 and higher
DOCUMENTS:
  • Driving license 2 sides
  • Passport front side
  • License (if any)
  • Phone number
  • Card number for transferring money (indicate in the letter)!
